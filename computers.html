I Enjoy Working With Computers

I've had a computer since the mid 80's. At first I used it mainly to play games. One day in the late 90's my husband introduced me the internet and GeoCities; I was hooked. I got a site on GeoCities, and learned basic HTML. In the early 2000's I discovered POGO an online gaming website and enjoy playing the many games it offers. Now I am learning to program. The internet has many free sources to learn programming. One of the best sites is edX. My goal is to have a website that has online games that I have written.
